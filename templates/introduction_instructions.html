Introduction Round Instructions

Overview

  • This is a voice-based introduction round
  • Total number of questions: 5
  • Format: Interactive conversation
  • Duration: Approximately 5-10 minutes

What to Expect

  • Basic introduction and greeting
  • Questions about your background
  • Discussion about your interests
  • Questions about your career goals
  • Why you're interested in this position

Microphone Test

Please test your microphone before starting the interview

Tips for Success

  • Speak clearly and at a moderate pace
  • Keep your responses concise but informative
  • Listen carefully to each question
  • Be professional and enthusiastic
  • Ensure you're in a quiet environment

Technical Setup

  • Use a modern browser (Chrome recommended)
  • Allow microphone access when prompted
  • Check your internet connection
  • Use headphones if possible
