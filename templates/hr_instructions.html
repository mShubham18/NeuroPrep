HR Round Instructions

Overview

  • This is a voice-based HR interview round
  • Total number of questions: 7
  • Format: Interactive conversation
  • Duration: Approximately 15-20 minutes

Question Types

  • Behavioral questions about past experiences
  • Situational judgment scenarios
  • Team collaboration examples
  • Problem-solving approaches
  • Career goals and aspirations

Tips for Success

  • Use the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result) for behavioral questions
  • Speak clearly and maintain a professional tone
  • Take a moment to think before responding
  • Provide specific examples from your experience
  • Be honest and authentic in your responses

Technical Requirements

  • Ensure your microphone is working properly
  • Test your audio settings before starting
  • Use a quiet environment with minimal background noise
  • Have a stable internet connection
